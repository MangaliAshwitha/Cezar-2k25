𝗝𝗡𝗧𝗨𝗛 𝗨𝗖𝗘𝗝

JNTUH University College of Engineering Jagtial (JNTUH UCEJ), established in 2007, is a premier engineering institution under Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH). Located in Jagitial, Telangana, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines like IT, Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, and Electronics Engineering. With a strong focus on academic excellence, research, and industry collaboration, JNTUH UCEJ provides students with hands-on learning, internships, and placement opportunities in leading companies. The campus fosters a culture of innovation, equipping students with industry-relevant skills while upholding traditional and cultural values.

𝗖𝗘𝗭𝗔𝗥 2025

CEZAR 2025 is a two-day national level technical symposium organized by JNTUH UCEJ. The mission is to produce technically trained superior human resources to serve present and future global needs by providing an excellent platform for interaction, knowledge sharing, and talent showcasing.

CEZAR 2K25 is a two-day national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Information Technology, JNTUH University College of Engineering Jagtial. Accredited with an NAAC A+ Grade, this event aims to foster innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among students. With an exciting lineup of technical events like Code Arena, Yukti Yudh, IdeaWave, Prakalp, and Fortune Trivia, as well as non-technical events including Treasure Hunt, Art Gallery, Flashmob, Esports, and Movie Mania, CEZAR 2K25 promises an engaging and competitive experience. Guided by esteemed faculty and dynamic student coordinators, the symposium is set to take place on April 9th and 10th, 2025. Be a part of this inspiring journey to explore, compete, and innovate!
